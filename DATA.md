# Data files

Large CSV and Excel datasets are **not** included in the GitHub repository (see `.gitignore`).

After cloning, place your data in this folder:

- **Single-file mode:** groundwater and rainfall CSVs in the project root (e.g. `DunTestData.csv`, `DunRain.csv`)
- **Folder mode:** well CSVs in `Waikato_data/` (or your own folder; update paths in `Gwldd.ipynb`)
- **Canterbury large-dataset mode:** files in `Canterbury_data/`

Update the paths in `Gwldd.ipynb` to match your machine.
